AstroFuture ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the AstroFuture mobile application.

By installing and using AstroFuture, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

ACCOUNT DATA
Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect: name, email address, date of birth, time of birth, city of birth, and gender. This information is used exclusively for generating accurate astrological calculations and personalized horoscope predictions.

Birth Details

Your birth information (date, time, and place) is used to calculate planetary positions, generate your Kundali chart, Dasha periods, and compatibility analysis. This data is stored securely and never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

AI Chat Data

When you chat with our AI astrologers, your messages and their responses are stored in our database to provide continuous conversation support. Chat history is associated with your account and protected under this policy.

Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication to manage user accounts securely. Your email and authentication credentials are handled by Firebase, governed by Google's privacy practices.

USAGE DATA
Local Cache

The app may cache horoscope and Panchang data on your device for faster access. This cached data can be cleared at any time from within the app's settings.

Push Notifications

If you enable daily horoscope notifications, we use Expo Notifications to deliver personalized reminders. Notification preferences can be changed or disabled at any time from the app settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Generating your Kundali (birth chart) and planetary position analysis
  • Calculating Vimshottari Dasha periods and transit predictions
  • Providing daily, weekly, and monthly horoscope readings
  • Delivering personalized AI astrology chat responses
  • Performing compatibility (Rashi Milan) analysis
  • Sending daily horoscope notifications (if enabled)
  • Calculating Numerology readings based on your birth data
  • Managing your account and providing customer support

3. Third-Party Services We Use

Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore to manage user accounts and store app data. Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Learn more at: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Anthropic (AI Responses)

Your chat messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI to generate astrological responses. Messages are sent to Anthropic's API solely for generating AI responses and are not used for training AI models. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their privacy policy.

Expo (Build & Notifications)

We use the Expo framework to build and distribute the app. Expo's privacy practices can be reviewed at: expo.io/privacy

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to advertisers, marketers, or any other third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore, which uses encryption in transit and at rest. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Chat history and horoscope data are stored as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your stored data through the app profile section
  • Edit: Update your name, birth details, and preferences at any time
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the app or by contacting us
  • Export: Request a copy of your data by contacting us via email
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications from the app settings without deleting your account

How to Delete Your Data

You can permanently delete your account and all data from within the app: Profile → Privacy & Data → Delete Account. This action is irreversible and will remove all personal information, chat history, and profile data from our servers.
